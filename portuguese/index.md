# Portuguese topic guide

## Lesson design preferences

Future Portuguese lessons in this topic should be optimized for the user, who learns best with quizzes and active recall.

Each new lesson should include:
- a short practical objective
- core phrases with English meaning
- simple pronunciation confidence hints for the most important words or phrases
- at least one mini dialogue
- a quiz section with recall or translation questions
- a small self-check or answer key
- encouragement to ask follow-up pronunciation questions

## Pronunciation guidance style

Use lightweight learner-friendly approximations, not dense IPA by default.

Preferred format:
- **queria** ≈ "keh-REE-ah"
- **desculpe** ≈ "desh-KOOL-peh"

Keep pronunciation notes clearly marked as approximations for confidence, not exact phonetic transcription.

## Quiz preference

Bias lessons toward:
- translation from English to Portuguese
- fill-in-the-blank prompts
- mini response drills
- short situational prompts like "You want the bill" or "You want water"
