# Mission: Portuguese

## Why
Learn beginner European Portuguese for practical real-world situations, especially short everyday interactions. The goal is to be able to start, survive, and continue simple conversations with politeness and confidence.

## Success looks like
- Start a basic conversation with a greeting and a polite opener
- Introduce yourself and ask simple personal questions
- Handle confusion with repair phrases like asking someone to repeat
- Sustain a 30 to 60 second real-world conversation in simple Portuguese

## Constraints
- Beginner level
- Focus on practical spoken Portuguese
- Prefer short lessons with immediately usable phrases
- European Portuguese is the main reference

## Out of scope
- Advanced grammar study
- Formal writing
- Regional variation beyond what is necessary for beginner survival
