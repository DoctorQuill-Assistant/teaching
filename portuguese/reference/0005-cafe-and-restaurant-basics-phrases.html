Portuguese Reference: Café and Restaurant Basics

Quick review sheet for useful beginner Portuguese in cafés and restaurants.

High-value phrases

  • Com licença. — Excuse me.
  • Queria um café. — I would like a coffee.
  • Queria dois pastéis de nata. — I would like two custard tarts.
  • Mais alguma coisa? — Anything else?
  • É tudo, obrigado/a. — That’s all, thank you.
  • A conta, por favor. — The bill, please.
  • Posso pagar com cartão? — Can I pay by card?

Useful words

  • café — coffee
  • água — water
  • chá — tea
  • sumo — juice
  • pastel de nata — custard tart
  • conta — bill
  • cartão — card

Pronunciation confidence hints

  • café ≈ "kah-FEH"
  • com licença ≈ "kong lee-SEN-sah"
  • queria ≈ "keh-REE-ah"
  • a conta, por favor ≈ "ah KON-tah pur fah-VOR"

Approximate help for confidence, not exact phonetics.

Mini self-check

  • the bill, please → a conta, por favor
  • anything else? → mais alguma coisa?
  • I would like two coffees. → Queria dois cafés.
