Portuguese Reference: Numbers, Prices, Time, and Quantities

Quick review sheet for the most useful beginner Portuguese numbers.

Numbers to know first

  • zero — zero
  • um / uma — one
  • dois / duas — two
  • três — three
  • quatro — four
  • cinco — five
  • dez — ten
  • onze — eleven
  • doze — twelve
  • vinte — twenty

High-value phrases

  • Quanto custa? — How much does it cost?
  • Custa cinco euros. — It costs five euros.
  • Queria dois cafés. — I would like two coffees.
  • Queria uma água. — I would like one water.
  • Que horas são? — What time is it?
  • São duas horas. — It is two o’clock.

Pronunciation confidence hints

  • um ≈ "oong"
  • dois ≈ "doysh"
  • três ≈ "treysh"
  • vinte ≈ "VEEN-t"
  • quanto custa ≈ "KWAN-too KOOSH-tah"

Approximate help for confidence, not exact phonetics.

Mini self-check

  • two coffees → dois cafés
  • five euros → cinco euros
  • It is three o’clock. → São três horas.
