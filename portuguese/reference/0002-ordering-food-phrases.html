Portuguese Reference: Ordering Food Phrases

Quick review sheet for beginner restaurant Portuguese.

Essential phrases

  • Desculpe. — Excuse me.
  • O menu, por favor. — The menu, please.
  • Queria... — I would like...
  • Queria o prato do dia. — I would like the dish of the day.
  • Água, por favor. — Water, please.
  • A conta, por favor. — The bill, please.
  • Posso pagar com cartão? — Can I pay by card?

Menu words

  • entrada — starter
  • prato do dia — dish of the day
  • sobremesa — dessert
  • peixe — fish
  • carne — meat
