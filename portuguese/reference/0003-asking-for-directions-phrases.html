Portuguese Reference: Asking for Directions

Quick review sheet with only the 4 new items from the simplified lesson.

Core phrases

  • Onde fica...? — Where is...?
  • Em frente. — Straight ahead.
  • À esquerda. — To the left.
  • À direita. — To the right.

Reuse from earlier lessons

  • Desculpe. — Excuse me.
  • Obrigado/a. — Thank you.

Pronunciation confidence hints

  • onde fica ≈ "OHN-dj fih-kah"
  • em frente ≈ "eng FREHN-t"
  • à esquerda ≈ "ah esh-KEHR-dah"
  • à direita ≈ "ah dee-RAY-tah"

Approximate help for confidence, not exact phonetics.

Mini self-check

  • Where is the hotel? → Onde fica o hotel?
  • straight ahead → em frente
  • to the left → à esquerda
