Asking for directions

In this reworked lesson, you will focus on just a few high-value direction phrases. The goal is to ask where something is and understand three simple answers without overload.

Focus: practical beginner Portuguese with a low-memory load.

New vocabulary in this lesson: only 4 items. We reuse earlier polite phrases like desculpe and obrigado/a.

What success looks like

  • Ask where a place is with one simple pattern.
  • Understand three common direction answers.
  • Practice speaking without needing lots of new words.

Today’s 4 new items

Onde fica...? = Where is...?
Em frente. = Straight ahead.
À esquerda. = To the left.
À direita. = To the right.

That is all the new core material for this lesson.

The key beginner pattern

Onde fica...? = Where is...?

Use it with familiar place words. You do not need to memorize many new place names right now.

Onde fica o hotel? = Where is the hotel?
Onde fica o restaurante? = Where is the restaurant?
Onde fica o café? = Where is the café?
Desculpe, onde fica o hotel? = Excuse me, where is the hotel?

Mini dialogue 1

You: Desculpe, onde fica o hotel?

Local: Em frente.

You: Obrigado/a.

Mini dialogue 2

You: Desculpe, onde fica o restaurante?

Local: À esquerda.

You: Obrigado/a.

Mini dialogue 3

You: Desculpe, onde fica o café?

Local: À direita.

You: Obrigado/a.

Pronunciation confidence hints

  • onde fica ≈ "OHN-dj fih-kah"
  • em frente ≈ "eng FREHN-t"
  • à esquerda ≈ "ah esh-KEHR-dah"
  • à direita ≈ "ah dee-RAY-tah"

These are learner-friendly confidence hints, not exact phonetics.

Memory-friendly note

You do not need to memorize lots of place words yet. Practice one question pattern and three direction answers first. Repetition matters more than volume.

What to memorize first

  1. Onde fica...?
  2. Em frente.
  3. À esquerda.
  4. À direita.

Quiz 1: English to Portuguese

  1. Where is...?
  2. Straight ahead.
  3. To the left.
  4. To the right.

Quiz 2: Fill in the blank

  1. Onde _____ ...?
  2. Em _____.
  3. À _____.
  4. À direita. = to the _____.

Quiz 3: Mini response drills

  1. You want to ask where the hotel is.
  2. Someone tells you: straight ahead. Say it in Portuguese.
  3. You want to ask where the café is.
  4. Someone tells you: to the left. Say it in Portuguese.

Speaking drill

Say these aloud three times each:

  1. Onde fica o hotel?
  2. Onde fica o restaurante?
  3. Em frente.
  4. À esquerda.
  5. À direita.

Self-check answer key

  • Where is...? → Onde fica...?
  • Straight ahead. → Em frente.
  • To the left. → À esquerda.
  • To the right. → À direita.
  • Where is the hotel? → Onde fica o hotel?
