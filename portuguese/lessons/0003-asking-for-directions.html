Asking for directions

In this lesson, you will learn how to ask where something is, understand simple direction words, and respond when someone tells you to go straight, left, or right.

Focus: practical beginner Portuguese for getting around in Portugal.

Ask follow-up questions any time. Your teacher can help with pronunciation, street language, or extra direction drills.

What success looks like

  • Ask where a place is in a polite way.
  • Understand key direction words like left, right, and straight ahead.
  • Recognize simple location phrases like next to and on the other side of the street.
  • Use a few survival phrases if you do not understand the answer.

The key beginner question

Onde fica...? = Where is...?

This is one of the most useful beginner travel patterns in Portuguese.

Onde fica a estação? = Where is the station?
Onde fica o hotel? = Where is the hotel?
Onde fica a farmácia? = Where is the pharmacy?
Desculpe, onde fica o cinema? = Excuse me, where is the cinema?

Essential phrases

Desculpe. = Excuse me.
Onde fica...? = Where is...?
Como é que eu vou para...? = How do I get to...?
Em frente. = Straight ahead.
À esquerda. = To the left.
À direita. = To the right.
Ao lado de... = Next to...
Do outro lado da rua. = On the other side of the street.
Não percebo. = I don’t understand.
Pode repetir, por favor? = Can you repeat, please?

Useful place words

Places

  • estação — station
  • hotel — hotel
  • farmácia — pharmacy
  • restaurante — restaurant
  • cinema — cinema

Direction words

  • em frente — straight ahead
  • à esquerda — to the left
  • à direita — to the right
  • até ao fim da rua — until the end of the street
  • atravessa — cross

Location words

  • ao lado de — next to
  • do outro lado — on the other side
  • perto — near
  • longe — far
  • rua — street

Mini dialogue

You: Desculpe, onde fica o cinema?

Local: Vai sempre em frente até ao fim da rua.

Local: Depois vira à esquerda.

Local: O cinema fica ao lado da livraria.

You: Obrigado.

Pronunciation confidence hints

  • onde fica ≈ "OHN-dj fih-kah"
  • esquerda ≈ "esh-KEHR-dah"
  • direita ≈ "dee-RAY-tah"
  • em frente ≈ "eng FREHN-t"
  • repetir ≈ "reh-peh-TEER"

These are approximate confidence hints, not exact phonetics.

Survival tip

If someone answers too fast, use Não percebo. and Pode repetir, por favor? These two phrases are extremely useful in real street conversations.

What to memorize first

  1. Desculpe.
  2. Onde fica...?
  3. Em frente.
  4. À esquerda.
  5. Pode repetir, por favor?

Quick practice

Translate these into Portuguese:

  1. Excuse me.
  2. Where is the station?
  3. Straight ahead.
  4. To the left.
  5. Can you repeat, please?

Quiz

Say the Portuguese phrase you would use in each situation:

  1. You want to ask where the pharmacy is.
  2. You want to ask how to get to the hotel.
  3. You want to say “straight ahead”.
  4. You want to say “to the right”.
  5. You do not understand and want the person to repeat.

Speaking drill

Say these out loud three times each:

  1. Desculpe, onde fica a estação?
  2. Como é que eu vou para o hotel?
  3. Em frente.
  4. À esquerda.
  5. Pode repetir, por favor?

Aim for confidence and rhythm first.

Answer key

  • Where is the pharmacy? → Onde fica a farmácia?
  • How do I get to the hotel? → Como é que eu vou para o hotel?
  • Straight ahead. → Em frente.
  • To the right. → À direita.
  • Can you repeat, please? → Pode repetir, por favor?
