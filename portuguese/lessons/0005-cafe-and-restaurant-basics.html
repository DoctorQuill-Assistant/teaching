Café and restaurant basics

In this lesson, you will learn the most useful beginner Portuguese for cafés and restaurants: getting attention politely, ordering food and drinks, asking for the bill, and understanding a few common questions.

Focus: practical European Portuguese for real café and restaurant situations.

Ask follow-up questions any time. Your teacher can give more ordering drills, menu practice, or extra pronunciation help.

What success looks like

  • Order a drink or snack politely.
  • Say what you want with Queria...
  • Understand Mais alguma coisa?
  • Ask for the bill with confidence.

The key beginner patterns

Com licença. = Excuse me.
Queria um café, por favor. = I would like a coffee, please.
Mais alguma coisa? = Anything else?
A conta, por favor. = The bill, please.

These are the big survival phrases for cafés and restaurants.

Core phrases

Com licença. = Excuse me.
Queria um café. = I would like a coffee.
Queria dois pastéis de nata. = I would like two custard tarts.
Para mim, uma água. = For me, a water.
Mais alguma coisa? = Anything else?
É tudo, obrigado/a. = That’s all, thank you.
A conta, por favor. = The bill, please.
Posso pagar com cartão? = Can I pay by card?

Useful café words

  • café — coffee
  • água — water
  • chá — tea
  • sumo — juice
  • pão — bread
  • bolo — cake
  • pastel de nata — custard tart
  • conta — bill

Mini dialogue: ordering in a café

You: Com licença.

Server: Sim?

You: Queria um café e um pastel de nata, por favor.

Server: Mais alguma coisa?

You: É tudo, obrigado/a.

Mini dialogue: asking for the bill

You: Com licença, a conta, por favor.

Server: Claro.

Server: Como vai pagar?

You: Com cartão.

Politeness note

Quero is understandable, but Queria sounds softer and more polite in cafés and restaurants.

Com licença is great for getting attention politely. Desculpe is more like “sorry” or “excuse me” when you may have bothered someone.

Pronunciation confidence hints

  • café ≈ "kah-FEH"
  • água ≈ "AH-gwah"
  • com licença ≈ "kong lee-SEN-sah"
  • queria ≈ "keh-REE-ah"
  • mais alguma coisa? ≈ "mysh al-GOO-ma KOY-zah?"
  • a conta, por favor ≈ "ah KON-tah pur fah-VOR"
  • cartão ≈ "kar-TOWNG"

These are simple learner-friendly hints for confidence, not exact phonetics.

Speaking drill

Say these aloud three times:

  1. Com licença.
  2. Queria um café, por favor.
  3. Queria dois cafés.
  4. É tudo, obrigado/a.
  5. A conta, por favor.

Self-check answer key

  • Excuse me. → Com licença.
  • I would like a coffee. → Queria um café.
  • Anything else? → Mais alguma coisa?
  • That’s all, thank you. → É tudo, obrigado/a.
  • The bill, please. → A conta, por favor.
  • Can I pay by card? → Posso pagar com cartão?
