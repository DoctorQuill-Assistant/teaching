Portuguese • Lesson 2

Ordering food

In this lesson, you will learn how to order food politely in European Portuguese: ask for a menu, order food and drinks, say what you want, and ask for the bill.

Focus: practical restaurant Portuguese for beginners.

Ask follow-up questions any time. Your teacher can explain menu words, pronunciation, politeness, or restaurant culture in Portugal.

What success looks like

  • Get a server’s attention politely.
  • Ask for food or drinks using a natural beginner pattern.
  • Understand basic menu categories like starter, main dish, and dessert.
  • Ask for the bill at the end of the meal.

The key beginner pattern

Queria... = I would like...

This is one of the best beginner patterns because it sounds polite and works in many situations.

Queria o menu, por favor. = I would like the menu, please.
Queria um café. = I would like a coffee.
Queria o prato do dia. = I would like the dish of the day.
Queria a conta, por favor. = I would like the bill, please.

Essential restaurant phrases

Desculpe. = Excuse me.
O menu, por favor. = The menu, please.
Queria... = I would like...
Para beber... = To drink...
Sem... = Without...
Com... = With...
A conta, por favor. = The bill, please.
Posso pagar com cartão? = Can I pay by card?

Useful menu words

Course words

  • entrada — starter
  • prato do dia — dish of the day
  • sobremesa — dessert
  • acompanhamento — side dish

Food words

  • peixe — fish
  • carne — meat
  • pão — bread
  • azeitonas — olives

Drink and payment words

  • água — water
  • café — coffee
  • dinheiro — cash
  • cartão de crédito — credit card

Mini dialogue: ordering food

You: Desculpe. O menu, por favor.

Server: Claro.

You: Queria o prato do dia.

Server: E para beber?

You: Água, por favor.

Server: Mais alguma coisa?

You: Não, obrigado.

Mini dialogue: asking for the bill

You: Desculpe. A conta, por favor.

Server: Como vai pagar?

You: Com cartão.

Restaurant culture note

In Portugal, café usually means a small espresso, not a large filter coffee. If you just ask for um café, expect espresso.

Pronunciation confidence hints

  • queria ≈ “keh-REE-ah”
  • desculpe ≈ “desh-KOOL-peh”
  • sobremesa ≈ “soh-breh-MAY-zah”
  • conta ≈ “KOHN-tah”

These are confidence approximations, not exact phonetics.

What to memorize first

  1. Desculpe.
  2. O menu, por favor.
  3. Queria...
  4. Água, por favor.
  5. A conta, por favor.

Quick practice
