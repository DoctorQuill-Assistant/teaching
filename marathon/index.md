# Marathon topic guide

## Lesson design preferences

Future marathon lessons in this topic should be optimized for the user, who wants practical coaching and clear next steps.

Each new lesson should include:
- a short practical objective
- a few key concepts only
- simple explanations without too much jargon
- an actionable takeaway for training
- at least one mini example, scenario, or sample week when useful
- a short quiz or self-check section
- encouragement to ask follow-up questions

## Coaching style

Use concise, practical coaching language.

Preferred tone:
- calm
- supportive
- realistic
- focused on consistency and injury avoidance
