# Resources

1. Lawless French, "Restaurant"
   https://www.lawlessfrench.com/vocabulary/restaurant/

Used for common waiter/customer phrases, restaurant vocabulary, and the note that **la carte** means menu while **le menu** often refers to a fixed-price meal.

2. Lawless French, "Greetings"
   https://www.lawlessfrench.com/vocabulary/greetings/

Used for greeting etiquette, especially the importance of saying **bonjour**, and the distinction between **bonjour / bonsoir** and **bonne journée / bonne soirée**.

3. Lawless French, "Introductions"
   https://www.lawlessfrench.com/vocabulary/introductions/

Used for **je m'appelle**, polite name questions, and basic introduction patterns.

4. Lawless French, "Essential French"
   https://www.lawlessfrench.com/vocabulary/essential-french/

Used as a general beginner vocabulary cross-check for survival phrases and foundational expressions.
