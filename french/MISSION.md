# Mission

Teach practical, confidence-building French for real-world situations.

Current focus: help the user order dinner in French with polite phrases, simple restaurant vocabulary, and a short usable dialogue.

Teaching preferences for this topic:
- Make lessons quiz-forward, with active recall in every lesson.
- Include pronunciation support for all key phrases.
- Prefer a mix of short quizzes: fill-in-the-blank, translation, and multiple choice.
- When useful, include IPA plus a simple English-friendly pronunciation guide.
- Add brief notes for tricky sounds such as French R, nasal vowels, and silent final letters.
