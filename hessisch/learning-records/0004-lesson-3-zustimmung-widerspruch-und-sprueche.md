# Lesson 3 added

A third Hessisch lesson was added with a practical spoken focus on sounding more natural in conversation through casual agreement, softer contradiction, and short humorous sayings.

Lesson focus:
- `Jo freilisch` for natural confident agreement
- `Ei eben` for quick agreement or emphasis
- `Joa, kommt druff aa` for cautious flexible answers
- `Naa, so aa net` for softer contradiction
- `Mach kaa Fasele` as a playful mild rebuke
- `Des isses ja grad` for making a point
- `Ei, mach dich net nass` as a light calming phrase

The lesson keeps the broad understandable Südhessisch track and continues building tone and conversational rhythm rather than dense grammar.
