# Lesson 1 added

A first Hessisch lesson was added with a tight beginner scope: greeting casually, reacting naturally, and sounding more hessisch with a few high-utility Südhessisch forms.

Lesson focus:
- `Gude` as the main casual greeting
- `Ei` as a common hessisch conversation opener
- small but strong starter forms such as `isch`, `net`, `des`, and `mer`
- `babbele` as an everyday verb for chatting or talking
- one practical calming / humorous expression: `Ei, jetzt mach emol halblang`

The lesson was kept broadly understandable for Südhessisch learners rather than narrowly hyper-local.
