# Lesson 2 added

A second Hessisch lesson was added with a practical spoken focus on short everyday reactions, soft disagreement, and small hessisch filler words that make speech sound more natural.

Lesson focus:
- `jo` and `ei jo` for casual agreement
- `naa` for softer conversational disagreement
- `Ei, hör mer uff` as a flexible surprised / annoyed / playful reaction
- `Des glaab isch net` as simple contradiction
- `Ei, was e Geschiss` as a mild humorous complaint
- `Ei, jetzt bleib emol locker` as a useful calming expression

The lesson continues the broad, understandable Südhessisch track rather than using narrow hyper-local spellings.
