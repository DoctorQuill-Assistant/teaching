# Resources

Sources currently used for Hessisch lesson development:

1. Südhessisches Wörterbuch / LAGIS Hessen
- https://www.lagis-hessen.de/shwb
- Reference source for Südhessisch vocabulary, variants, and usage regions.

2. Lars Vorberger, *Hessisch: Vom Babbeln und Schnuddeln* (Duden, excerpt)
- https://shop.duden.de/media/bb/4f/cb/1687427174/Leseprobe_9783411756735_Hessisch.pdf
- Useful for broad orientation on Hessian dialect areas and common pronunciation tendencies such as dropped endings, softened consonants, and forms like `Gude`, `Daaach`, `Morje`, `awe/awer`, and `babbele`.

3. PONS overview page on Hessian words
- https://de.pons.com/p/wissensecke/wortschatz-to-go/hessische-woerter
- Lightweight supplementary source for common cultural words and idiomatic examples.

Usage notes:
- Favor broadly understandable Südhessisch over exaggerated spelling.
- Where exact Wiesbaden-area forms are uncertain, label them as broadly understandable Südhessisch rather than hyper-local Wiesbadener speech.
- Keep learner pronunciation hints simple and confidence-oriented.
