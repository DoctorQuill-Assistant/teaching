# Corrected “I am fine” phrase in early Tulu lessons

The earlier lesson incorrectly glossed `Yān kusald aande.` as “I am fine.”

For beginner self-introductions and simple status responses, prefer:
- `Yān ushar ulle.`
- `Yān saukya ulle.`

Also keep a note in `RESOURCES.md` so future lessons do not repeat the `kusal` mistake.
