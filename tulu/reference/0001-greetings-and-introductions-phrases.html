Tulu Reference: Greetings and Introductions

Quick review sheet for useful beginner Tulu opener phrases.

High-value phrases

  • Namaskāra. — Hello.
  • Ninna pudar enchina? — What is your name?
  • Enna pudar Maya. — My name is Maya.
  • Yān ushar ulle. — I am fine.
  • Yān saukya ulle. — I am fine.
  • I ench ullar? — How are you?
  • Daye malpule. — Please help.
  • Mast upakāra. — Thank you very much.

Pronunciation confidence hints

  • Namaskāra ≈ "nah-muhs-KAA-ruh"
  • ninna pudar enchina? ≈ "NIN-nuh POO-dur EN-chi-nuh?"
  • enna pudar ≈ "EN-nuh POO-dur"
  • yān ushar ulle ≈ "yaan OO-shur OOL-leh"
  • yān saukya ulle ≈ "yaan SOW-kya OOL-leh"

Approximate help for confidence, not exact phonetics.

Mini self-check

  • hello → namaskāra
  • my name is Ravi → enna pudar Ravi
  • how are you? → i ench ullar?
