Tulu Reference: Everyday Polite Phrases

Quick review sheet for useful polite beginner Tulu phrases.

High-value phrases

  • Mast upakāra. — Thank you very much.
  • Daye malpule. — Please help.
  • Avu. — Okay / yes.
  • Ijji. — No.
  • Enk artha apuji. — I do not understand.
  • Bokka oripule. — Please say it again. (learner-support approximation)
  • Dāye, nidāna pander. — Please speak slowly. (learner-support approximation)

Pronunciation confidence hints

  • mast upakāra ≈ "muhst oo-puh-KAA-ruh"
  • daye malpule ≈ "DAH-yeh mahl-poo-leh"
  • avu ≈ "AH-voo"
  • ijji ≈ "IJ-jee"
  • artha apuji ≈ "AR-thuh ah-poo-jee"

Approximate help for confidence, not exact phonetics.

Mini self-check

  • thank you very much → mast upakāra
  • please help → daye malpule
  • I do not understand → enk artha apuji
