Tulu Reference: Numbers

Quick review sheet for beginner counting in Tulu.

1–10

  • onji — 1
  • raDD — 2
  • mUji — 3
  • nAl — 4
  • ain — 5
  • Aji — 6
  • El — 7
  • enma — 8
  • orumba — 9
  • patt — 10

Useful extras

  • pattonji — 11
  • padiraDD — 12
  • padimUji — 13
  • padinAl — 14
  • padinain — 15

Useful number phrases

  • raDD kai — two hands
  • nAl ill — four houses
  • patt kilo ari — ten kilos of rice
  • enk raDD kai uNDu — I have two hands

Pronunciation confidence hints

  • onji ≈ "ON-jee"
  • raDD ≈ "rahdd"
  • mUji ≈ "MOO-jee"
  • orumba ≈ "oh-RUM-buh"
  • patt ≈ "puhtt"
